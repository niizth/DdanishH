BMI Categories

Understanding your BMI is crucial for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Here are the different BMI categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI > 30
